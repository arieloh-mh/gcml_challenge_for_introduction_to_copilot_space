# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing

### Role Summary
QA/Testing specialists ensure quality, validate features against acceptance criteria, and help maintain reliability. They collaborate with developers to catch issues early and improve testability.

### Responsibilities
- Define test plans and test cases from requirements
- Execute manual and automated tests
- Identify, document, and verify bug fixes
- Participate in acceptance criteria definition
- Advocate for quality in design and code reviews

### Goals
- Ensure features meet acceptance criteria before release
- Reduce defects in production
- Improve test coverage and automation

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Bug reports and test case documentation
- Collaboration with developers on testability

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers drive user-centric design by creating intuitive, accessible interfaces. They conduct user research, iterate on prototypes, and ensure the product meets usability standards.

### Responsibilities
- Design user experiences and interfaces that meet usability and accessibility standards
- Conduct user research, usability testing, and iterate on prototypes
- Create wireframes, mockups, and design specifications
- Collaborate with Product Managers to understand user needs
- Support Developers in implementing design accurately
- Work with QA on accessibility and UX testing

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Ensure designs align with user needs and business goals
- Maintain design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers and Developers
- User research findings and usability reports
- Design specifications and style guides

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate to clarify user needs and validate design solutions
- **Developers**: Support implementation with design specs and answer questions
- **QA/Testing**: Partner on accessibility and usability testing
- **Business Analysts**: Understand workflows and user journeys

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers maintain and improve deployment pipelines, CI/CD practices, and system reliability. They automate testing and deployment, monitor production systems, and participate in incident management.

### Responsibilities
- Design, maintain, and improve CI/CD pipelines
- Automate build, test, and deployment processes
- Monitor system performance, reliability, and availability
- Implement infrastructure as code and configuration management
- Participate in incident response and post-mortem reviews
- Ensure security best practices in deployment pipelines

### Goals
- Reduce deployment friction and time-to-production
- Maintain high system availability and reliability
- Enable rapid, safe releases through automation

### Typical Communication
- Deployment status and pipeline health updates
- Incident reports and post-mortems
- Infrastructure and tooling documentation

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Partner on build/test automation and deployment strategies
- **QA/Testing**: Collaborate on automated testing in pipelines
- **Project Managers**: Inform about release risks and deployment timelines
- **Security Specialist**: Coordinate on security scanning and compliance

---

## Security Specialist

### Role Summary
Security Specialists identify and mitigate security risks, perform security audits, ensure compliance, and respond to security incidents. They help maintain secure coding practices and protect the organization from threats.

### Responsibilities
- Identify, assess, and mitigate security risks
- Perform security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Ensure compliance with security policies and regulations
- Review code and architecture for security vulnerabilities
- Respond to and investigate security incidents
- Provide security training and guidance to teams

### Goals
- Protect systems and data from security threats
- Ensure compliance with security standards
- Foster a security-conscious culture across teams

### Typical Communication
- Security assessment reports and risk registers
- Vulnerability findings and remediation guidance
- Security incident reports and post-mortems

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Advise on secure coding practices and review implementations
- **DevOps Engineers**: Collaborate on security scanning in CI/CD pipelines
- **Product Managers**: Escalate critical security risks and compliance requirements
- **Project Managers**: Inform about security-related dependencies and timelines

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business stakeholders and technical teams. They analyze business needs, map workflows, gather requirements, and ensure solutions align with organizational goals.

### Responsibilities
- Analyze business requirements and translate them into clear specifications
- Map business processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Facilitate communication between stakeholders and technical teams
- Document workflows, use cases, and business rules
- Support Product Managers in backlog refinement
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business needs

### Goals
- Ensure technical solutions align with business objectives
- Improve clarity and reduce ambiguity in requirements
- Bridge communication gaps between business and technical teams

### Typical Communication
- Business requirements documents and user stories
- Stakeholder interviews and workshop facilitation
- Process flow diagrams and use case documentation

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate on requirements definition and prioritization
- **Project Managers**: Support planning with detailed requirements and estimates
- **Developers**: Provide clarification on business logic and requirements
- **UX Designers**: Share insights on user workflows and business processes
